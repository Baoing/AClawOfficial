[Dronecameraus](https://dronecameraus.com/) sells DJI drones, handheld cameras, action cameras, and accessories to US shoppers, with clear positioning as an authorized retail experience. The engagement focused on **full-site customization**: a bespoke theme direction, reusable sections for promos and collections, and storefront UX that supports deep catalogs and campaign velocity.

We aligned the build to how shoppers actually browse—series-first navigation, strong product rails, and trust content where purchase anxiety is highest—while keeping the theme maintainable as the catalog changes.

### Challenge

#### Specialist catalog without a fragile editor

- **Campaign cadence:** Seasonal and promo blocks needed to ship quickly without one-off code drops.
- **Mobile discovery:** Many sessions start on smaller screens; nested menus and PLPs had to stay usable.
- **Trust and policies:** Returns, warranty, and authenticity messaging needed consistent, credible placement.

### Our Solution

#### Custom theme + repeatable patterns

- **Section library** for heroes, promos, and editorial storytelling with documented settings.
- **Template strategy** for collections and product detail that scales across many device families.
- **Performance-aware media** patterns appropriate to image-heavy hardware merchandising.

### Technologies Used

#### Stack

- **Shopify:** Online Store 2.0, Liquid, JSON templates, Theme Check–friendly structure
- **UX:** Navigation depth, policy and support surfaces, conversion-focused PLP and PDP patterns
- **Content:** Blog and buying-guide entry points integrated into the storefront IA
