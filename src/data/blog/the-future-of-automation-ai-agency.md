### From “hands” to “systems”

Agencies that only ticket small theme tweaks will compress. Value moves to **architecture**: markets, data models, app boundaries, and performance budgets that survive peak.

### Productized audits and playbooks

Clients want fixed-scope diagnostics—**store diagnosis**, performance baselines, app rationalization—before retainers. Package what you already do verbally.

### AI as accelerator, not a substitute for review

Copilots help draft Liquid, GraphQL, and internal runbooks faster. Humans still sign off on **scopes, checkout changes, and anything touching money**.

### Partnering with merchants on ops literacy

Teach clients to read Flow, webhooks, and error dashboards. Agencies that hoard operational knowledge create brittle relationships.

### What stays uniquely human

Judgment under ambiguity, negotiation with finance and legal, and taste in merchandising. Automate the rest where Shopify gives durable APIs.
