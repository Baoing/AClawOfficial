**GEO** (generative engine optimization) is not magic—it is **information design** for a world where shoppers ask **ChatGPT-style tools** about your brand. Those systems reward **clear, consistent, verifiable** facts on pages they can fetch—not hidden PDFs or contradictory microcopy.

We map **which questions** hurt you if answered wrong (shipping regions, warranty windows, compatibility claims), then align **policy pages, PDP modules, and FAQ metaobjects** so humans and machines read the same source of truth.

We stay conservative on **claims**: we help you structure content so marketing can move fast **without** inventing certifications or inventory stories models will repeat.

### What it does

- **Workshops** with marketing, legal, and support to inventory “high-risk questions.”
- **Content templates** and metaobject models for approved answers and variants by market.
- **Measurement**—what to watch in Search Console, referral logs, and branded query reports as behaviors shift.
- **Integration** with your **SEO roadmap** so technical fixes and GEO content ship together.

### Who it's for

- Brands in **competitive categories** where assistants summarize many competitors at once.
- Global merchants whose **policy** differs by region and must not collapse into one vague paragraph.
- Teams already publishing great blogs that **never surface** in structured Q&A— we connect narrative to machine-legible patterns.
