We scope **AI applications** as **workflow software** with a model inside: there is still **UI, permissions, logging, and an approval path**. That keeps “AI” from becoming an unmonitored tab where anyone can paste customer PII.

We favor **human-in-the-loop** shipping: drafts are visible before send, locked fields stay locked, and risky intents route to supervisors.

Where Shopify provides **hooks** (Flow, Inbox, admin extensions), we use them first so you are not maintaining a bespoke stack for problems Shopify already partially solves.

### What it does

- **Use-case discovery** with CX, merchandising, and marketing—ranked by ROI and risk.
- **Prototype to production** for internal tools or customer-facing assistants with clear SLAs.
- **Training** for prompt patterns and red lines your team can reuse.
- **Quarterly reviews** of drift, revert rates, and incident learnings.

### Who it's for

- Merchants who tried **consumer chatbots** and got burned on accuracy.
- Brands ready to invest in **governance** as much as model choice.
- Teams that want **AI Clawers** to stay involved through tuning—not only launch day.
