We build themes as **products**: predictable sections, documented settings, and guardrails so **merchandising can ship** without opening pull requests for every banner swap.

Whether you need a **net-new theme**, a **surgical refactor**, or rescue of a **vendor-delivered mess**, we start from inventory of apps, scripts, and metafields—then propose an architecture that will not collapse when Black Friday traffic arrives.

Performance is not an afterthought: we set **LCP budgets** on collection and PDP templates, audit **image pipelines**, and reduce **render-blocking** third parties. SEO-friendly patterns for **pagination, facets, and canonicals** are baked in—not patched later.

### What it does

- **New OS 2.0 themes** from approved designs, with componentized sections and presets.
- **Customization** of existing commercial themes where licensing and upgrade paths allow.
- **Technical debt reduction**—removing dead scripts, consolidating CSS, and clarifying data sources.
- **Launch support**—theme editor training and short hypercare window after go-live.

### Who it's for

- Heads of ecommerce who need **velocity and stability** at the same time.
- Brands with **multi-region** or **multi-brand** storefronts that share components.
- Teams that want **Theme Check–clean** repos and sensible Git workflows.
