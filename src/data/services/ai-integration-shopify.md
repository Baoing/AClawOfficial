“AI integration” should mean **measurable leverage**: faster **correct** GraphQL, safer **customer-facing** answers, or **support** drafts grounded in order facts—not a chat bubble that **guesses** discounts.

We separate **developer acceleration** (AI Toolkit, Dev MCP, Theme Check habits) from **shopper-facing** pilots (Storefront MCP, tool-grounded agents) because the **risk profile and testing bar** are different.

Every engagement includes **fallbacks**: what happens when a model refuses, a tool times out, or inventory disagrees with the cache—so you never silently promise what you cannot fulfill.

### What it does

- **Assessments** of where AI actually saves time vs creates new review work.
- **Implementation support** for official Shopify paths and your chosen models or hosts.
- **Evaluation harnesses**—test sets for prompts, regression scripts for tool-calling paths.
- **Governance** documentation: roles, approvals, logging, and retention aligned to your privacy policy.

### Who it's for

- Teams who read Shopify’s AI announcements and need a **sober implementation map**.
- Merchants who already have **data discipline** (metafields, policies) and want AI to amplify it.
- Organizations that must involve **security and legal** before turning on new customer channels.
