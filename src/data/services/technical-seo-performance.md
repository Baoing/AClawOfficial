Technical SEO on Shopify is **systems work**: how **collections, filters, tags, and apps** interact with **crawl budget** and **canonical signals**. We pair Search Console and crawl data with **theme and app reality**—not generic checklists.

Performance and SEO are linked: **slow mobile templates** hurt both conversion and crawl efficiency. We prioritize fixes on **high-impression URL templates**, then document **guardrails** so the next campaign does not undo the win with a 5 MB hero asset.

We work with your **content and legal** stakeholders so structured data and on-page claims stay **verifiable**—especially for regulated categories.

### What it does

- **Audits and prioritized backlogs** with effort vs impact scoring.
- **Implementation** in theme or app layers, plus validation in staging and production.
- **Monitoring** setup—alerts for coverage drops, CWV regressions, and broken redirect chains.
- **Training** so your editors know which fields affect snippets and internal link graphs.

### Who it's for

- Ecommerce managers who need **SEO and speed** on the same roadmap as launches.
- Brands recovering from a **replatform** or major **theme overhaul**.
- Teams where **paid and organic** share PDP templates—fixes must not break either channel.
