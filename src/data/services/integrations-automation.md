Integrations are where **quiet revenue leaks** turn into **loud incidents**: double charges, wrong inventory, or customers told an item ships when it does not. We design pipelines assuming **retries, duplicates, and delayed webhooks** are normal—not edge cases.

We document **source of truth** per entity (SKU, location, price, customer tags) and build **reconciliation views** so support and ops can answer “why does Shopify say X while the warehouse says Y?” without a war room.

We use **Shopify Flow** when it is the right complexity—not every automation deserves custom infrastructure.

### What it does

- **Integration architecture** workshops with your technical owners and vendors.
- **Implementation** of workers, queues, and admin visibility for non-engineers.
- **Cutover planning** for high-risk switches (3PL change, ERP swap) with rollback paths.
- **Runbooks** your team can execute without paging us for every exception.

### Who it's for

- Heads of operations who need **inventory truth** more than another dashboard.
- Merchants adding **B2B**, **subscriptions**, or **marketplaces** that complicate fulfillment logic.
- Teams tired of paying for **Zapier chains** that nobody dares to touch.
