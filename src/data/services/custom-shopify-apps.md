Custom apps are the right tool when **business rules, secrets, or multi-system orchestration** cannot live safely in the theme alone. We scope **Admin vs checkout vs storefront-adjacent** surfaces explicitly, because each has different **constraints, review paths, and blast radius**.

We design APIs and data flows for **observability**: structured logs, retries, dead-letter handling, and dashboards your ops team can read when a 3PL event disagrees with Shopify inventory.

For **App Store** targets we bake in **billing, GDPR-friendly data minimization**, and **reviewer-friendly UX** early—not as a scramble the week before submission.

### What it does

- **Discovery and technical design** — entities, scopes, rate limits, and failure modes on a diagram you can share internally.
- **Implementation** — Node or your preferred stack aligned to Shopify’s reference patterns.
- **QA** — automated tests where valuable, plus manual checkout and edge-case scripts.
- **Handover** — runbooks, environment variables, and on-call expectations for your team or ours.

### Who it's for

- Merchants who tried **five apps** and still do not have the workflow they need.
- Engineering leads who want **Shopify-native** patterns instead of a fragile external “middleware only” posture.
- Brands launching **innovation** that must coexist with peak trading periods.
